About

Simplifying your job search one newsletter at a time.

New Jobs Friday UK is a weekly newsletter that delivers all the latest job offers in the UK music industry straight to your inbox every Friday.

It all started as a script I wrote back in 2021 when I was looking for a new job in the music industry and I got fed up with having to check all the different music career websites individually and receiving newsletters with offers from all over the world.

During the Summer of 2022, after getting a new job, I decided to turn this script into a free newsletter called New Jobs Friday UK. The goal was simple - send an email with a list of all the latest job offers in the UK Music Industry from the most popular career websites.
